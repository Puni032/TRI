About the Institute

The Temple of Science and Technology (TeST) is a global, inclusive research institute dedicated to seeking universal truth through science. Open to all—regardless of nationality, race, or religion—TeST merges ethical inquiry, mindful innovation, and sustainable technologies to benefit humanity and the planet.

Research Highlights

  • Sustainable Energy Systems: Exploring green energy solutions that are scalable and globally accessible.
  • Mindful AI & Ethics: Developing artificial intelligence grounded in ethical awareness and human-centered values.
  • Global Health Innovation: Applying science to eliminate disparities in healthcare access and delivery.
  • Cosmology & Consciousness: Investigating the intersection of physics, metaphysics, and universal awareness.
